As a lifelong learner with a strong passion for innovation and solving problems using first principles, I believe in being the change I desire to see in the world. My journey spans from teaching high-level Mathematics in UAE schools to spearheading online & blended learning techniques during the COVID-19 pandemic.

Today, as CEO of B&B Creators, I lead a rapidly growing digital agency that empowers brands to scale through strategic online presence and cutting edge digital solutions. Under my direction, we also deliver bespoke AI-powered tools across education, public health, enterprise, and consumer sectors. Notably, my work led to the creation of African Intelligence; Africa's first open source, community-driven learning management system powered by artificial intelligence.
